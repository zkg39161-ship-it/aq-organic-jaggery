About AQ Fruit Farm

AQ Fruit Farm is a premium natural food provider, offering organic jaggery and other natural products using traditional and clean methods. Our products are completely free from chemicals, preservatives, and artificial colors, ensuring purity and health in every bite.


We work directly with farmers to maintain quality, sustainability, and authenticity. The production process preserves essential nutrients, making AQ Fruit Farm products a healthier alternative to processed foods. Our mission is to promote a natural lifestyle through clean, organic, and trustworthy food products.
